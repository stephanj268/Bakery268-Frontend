About Us

Where craft meets
every crumb

Shoye's Banana Bread was born from a simple belief: that the best food is made slowly, intentionally, and with real ingredients you can name. We source ripe, fragrant bananas and pair them with quality butter, free-range eggs, and unrefined sugars to create loaves that are as nourishing as they are indulgent.

Every batch is mixed and baked by hand in small quantities — so what arrives at your door is always fresh, always consistent, and always made with the same quiet pride that started it all.
